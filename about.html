This is our first text based adventure game, inspired by various movies.

Such as 28 Days Later and War Of The Worlds, based upon an alien apocolypse.

You find yourself in a chair sat at a desk, bewildered and lost.

The choices you make will directly impact the outcome of other scenarios you may face along the way....


Can you escape and unravel the truth?
